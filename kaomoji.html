Kaomoji

Kaomoji, also known as Japanese emoticons (йЎ”ж–Үеӯ—), are a popular style of emoticons originating from Japan.


They consist of Japanese characters and punctuation marks, and are commonly used to express emotions in text messages and online communication.


The term "kaomoji" is synonymous with Japanese emoticons and combines the Kanji words "kao" (йЎ” - "face") and "moji" (ж–Үеӯ— - "character").
